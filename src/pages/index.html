2020 is off to a great start here at Touchlab! We're kicking off the year with new clients looking to fast-track their KMP programs and ship more shared code! This week's newsletter includes registration for our Touchlab KaMP Kit (special thank you to our early testers for providing feedback in preparation for the kit's official launch). We also continue our series from last year on Kotlin Native concurrency with Parts 2 and 3. Have a great Monday and week!

Practical Kotlin Native Concurrency (Pt. 2)

In Part 2, Kevin goes into greater detail about some of the concepts and approaches presented in Practical Kotlin Native Concurrency Part 1.

He discusses global state, creates a simple background thread processor and explains some debugging techniques.

Practical Kotlin Native Concurrency (Pt. 3)

In Part 3, we look at atomics and actual concurrency libraries. These include the multithreaded coroutines preview, CoroutineWorker, and Reaktive.

Plenty of great stuff!
